## Overview

Final Draft Advanced is the industry-standard screenwriting application that handles screenplay formatting automatically, enabling writers to focus on story and dialogue while the software manages the precise formatting rules that the film and television industries require. It has been the standard screenwriting tool in Hollywood for three decades, with its file format accepted by every major studio, agency, and production company.

Automatic formatting applies correct screenplay elements — scene headings, action, character names, dialogue, parentheticals, transitions — as the writer types, handling the complex formatting rules that screenwriting manuals specify. The formatting is intelligent enough to anticipate the next element based on context, making screenplay writing flow naturally without manual formatting.

Story planning tools include beat boards, outlining, and index card views that help writers structure their stories before and during the writing process. The planning views connect to the screenplay, enabling navigation between structural overview and detailed script writing. Tagging marks story elements for tracking across the screenplay.

Real-time collaboration enables multiple writers to work on the same script simultaneously with change tracking and commenting, handling the co-writing workflow that television writers' rooms and writing partnerships require.

For screenwriters, showrunners, and filmmakers who need their scripts formatted to industry standards and accepted by every studio and production company, Final Draft Advanced is the screenwriting tool that the entertainment industry standardizes on.

## Performance

Final Draft balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
